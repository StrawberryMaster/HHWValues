What is HHWValues?

HHWValues is a political compass test that is based on the Hypothetical Hurricanes Wiki community. It is a fork of the SapplyValues GitHub repository. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.
There are 27 questions in the test.
